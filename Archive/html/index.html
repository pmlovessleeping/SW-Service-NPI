NPI Planning Guide

SW Service NPI Guideline

A practical guide for HW PMs to engage SW PMs early, prepare the right inputs, and align software planning output during NPI.

Overview Timing Planning Flow Checklist Output

Purpose

When HW PM should engage SW PM

Use this guideline when

  • A new NPI project requires software service planning.
  • Sample quantity, configuration, or schedule is not yet clear.
  • The project may reuse an existing platform or introduce a new one.
  • HW PM needs SW PM input before committing the NPI plan.

Expected outcome

  • Clear SW request timing and required inputs.
  • Aligned sample preparation logic.
  • Lead time expectation for new and existing platforms.
  • A structured SW Planning Output that can be reviewed and tracked.

Request Timing

Engage SW PM before NPI assumptions become fixed

1

Initial NPI Planning

HW PM identifies whether software service, tool, image, automation, or validation support may be needed.

2

Pre-meeting Preparation

HW PM collects platform, schedule, sample, and dependency information before meeting SW PM.

3

SW Planning Review

HW PM and SW PM confirm scope, lead time, R&R, risks, and planning output.

4

NPI Plan Commitment

Agreed software assumptions are reflected in the NPI plan and tracked through execution.

Planning Flow

SW planning decision path

Confirm platform type

Classify the project as new platform, existing platform, or platform leading project.

→

→
Publish output

Sample Logic

Sample preparation criteria

Criteria Planning Question Output Needed
Platform Is this a new, existing, or leading platform? Platform classification and reuse assumption.
Configuration Which HW/SW configurations require validation? Sample matrix and priority.
Schedule When are samples available for SW work? Sample readiness date and risk notes.
Ownership Who prepares, ships, flashes, and maintains samples? HW PM / SW PM / team R&R.

Lead Time

Reference baseline

Existing platform

Use when the project reuses a known platform and software changes are limited.

Baseline: confirm with SW PM

New platform

Use when platform bring-up, new dependency, or new validation flow is required.

Baseline: requires early review

Platform leading project

Use when this project defines the reusable baseline for later projects.

Baseline: align roadmap impact

R&R

HW PM and SW PM responsibilities

Area HW PM SW PM
Request initiation Raise SW planning needs during NPI planning. Confirm SW engagement model and required inputs.
Sample planning Provide sample schedule, configuration, and quantity assumptions. Review sample suitability for SW work and validation.
Lead time Share NPI milestone expectations. Provide software planning lead time and risk notes.
Tracking Track NPI commitment and dependency closure. Track SW deliverables, blockers, and planning output updates.
